Odin's Lasagna Cupcakes

Description

This delicious Odin family recipe puts a new twist on the Italian favourite! Lasagna cupcakes are a great way to enjoy the dish on the go, or as a nibble at a party. Much less mess, same great taste!

Ingredients - Serves 4

  • Cooking Spray
  • 1⅓ pounds of ground beef
  • Salt and round black pepper to taste
  • 96 Wonton wrappers
  • Cheese:
    • 7 cups of grated parmesan cheese
    • 7 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups pasta sauce (such as Dolmio)
  • 1 cup chopped fresh basil, or to taste (optional)
